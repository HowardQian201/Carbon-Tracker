Carbon Tracker

About Our Product


Our website allows college students to visualize how their transportation choices affect both their health and the environment. A user inputs the location of their dorm, classes, meals, and other activities throughout the day, and then our system uses Google's API to find distances. It then calculate several metrics, including CO2 saved, calories burned, and distance traveled.
Designed by Howard Qian, Alan Tapper, Akash Karasan, and Albert Wan of Rice University
